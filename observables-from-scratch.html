Observables from Scratch

October 3rd, 2020

Observables are an implementation of the observer design pattern frequently associated with ReactiveX due to the rise in popularity of RxJS. However, many developers find RxJS and complain about its steep learning curve. RxJS doesn't have to be a mystery and knowing its history is the key. Therefore, this post will shed light on the motivation and intuition behind such a framework by implementing RxJS-style observables from scratch, starting from the first origins, by implementing an HTML button's event handler with four events:

  • mouseover
  • mouseout
  • mousedown
  • mouseup
